Accessibility

AWS Community Day Ahmedabad 2026 is committed to being accessible and inclusive for all attendees.

Venue Accessibility

  • The venue is wheelchair accessible with ramps and lifts.
  • Accessible restrooms are available throughout the venue.
  • Reserved seating areas for attendees with disabilities.
  • Clear signage and wayfinding throughout the venue.

Event Accommodations

  • If you require specific accommodations, please contact us in advance.
  • We will do our best to accommodate dietary restrictions at meals.
  • Quiet areas will be available for attendees who need a break.

Request Accommodations

Need specific accessibility support? Contact us at awsugahm@gmail.com and we'll do our best to help.
