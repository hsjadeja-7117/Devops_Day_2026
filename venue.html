About the Venue (AMA)

Ahmedabad Management Association (AMA) is a founder member of the All India Management Association (AIMA), actively collaborating in management education nationwide. Located in Ahmedabad's prime area, AMA's 30,000 sq. mt. complex, established in 1997, neighbours IIM, Gujarat University, and other prestigious institutions.

ISO 9001 certified and approved for Management Training by the Government of Gujarat, AMA has won the Best Local Management Association Award (Category-I) from AIMA a record 20 times (1990–2023). Over 57 years, AMA has grown to 400+ institutional and 3,000+ individual members, united to exchange ideas and enhance managerial excellence. All programmes are need-based and offered at nominal fees. AMA — Always Moving Ahead!

Directions: Located on Dr Vikram Sarabhai Marg in the University Area. The campus is a prominent landmark situated right next to IIM Ahmedabad.
